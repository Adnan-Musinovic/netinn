before we start

Stuff to note

We get asked a ton of questions day-to-day but there are some we get asked nearly every time. Most of them are important, some of them less so, but all of them give an insight to what it's like to work with us.

For the more serious stuff here are our full terms and how we handle your data.

Our name

Pronounced "netinn", our name originates from our love of working with like-minded people. We don’t hide our team behind project managers. During a project, you’ll create a friendship with us all. While chatting over your ideas and working through your designs; you’ll quickly discover what makes us tick.

Pricing

Project costs depend on the time it will take, and how much fun it will be to work on. There’s no price list,everything we produce is bespoke and tailored to your exact requirement. If you’ve got something fun to work on and a few quid to keep us in business,then we’d love to hear from you.

Timelines

We’re creative people, and we’re also people that like to do an outstanding job. Expect a project that includes branding and a website to last around twelve weeks. Things can get done quicker; we just prefer to under promise and over deliver. We’ll stick to your deadlines, but we hate to be rushed.

Spec Work

You can twist our arm, send us beers, and dangle the odd carrot or two but we won’t create conceptual work to win business, unless your paying. It’s just not part of our culture. We believe in a collaborative partnership where we listen, research, and work with you to create the perfect solution to meet your needs.
