why join us?

Have fun being creative again

We want you to be happy and healthy, so we reward our team by going that little be further, offering a sprinkle of something extra to keep the creativity flowing.

Live a little

We like our team to have a life outside of work so our standard working hours are 8 am till 4 pm Monday to Thursday, and 8 am till 2:30 pm on Friday’s. You can even choose your start and finishing time so long as your hours and work are getting done.

Never too late

Sometimes you might be called upon to put in additional hours to complete deadlines, but as our number one rule, we want everyone to avoid having to do this. There is a life away from a MacBook and screen.

Sun, sea, sand

As well as excellent working hours we give you 25 days holiday plus office closure between Christmas and New Year. This gives everyone time to re-charge those batteries and get those creative juices flowing again.

The little extras

We also reward all our team with a pension scheme, competitive salaries, free access to online learning, MacBooks, monthly team outings, and for the foodies weekly treats and the odd free breakfast.
